Your account and billingedit

Manage your Elasticsearch Service account right from the Elasticsearch Service Console. You can use the Account page to perform the following tasks:

  • Edit user account settings
  • Monitor and analyze your account usage
  • Add your billing details
  • Choose a subscription level
  • See your billing history
  • Update billing and operational contacts
  • Stop charges for a deployment

We also offer AWS Marketplace billing, GCP Marketplace billing and Azure Marketplace billing, if you prefer not to add a credit card directly.

Got more questions about billing for Elasticsearch Service? Read more about our usage-based billing dimensions or check the billing FAQ, which includes information on how to get in touch with us with questions about your bill.

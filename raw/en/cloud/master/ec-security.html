Securing your deploymentedit

Elasticsearch Service supports most of the security features that are part of the Elastic Stack. These features are designed to:

  • Prevent unauthorized access with password protection and role-based access control:

    • Reset the elastic user password.
    • Manage your users on different versions.
    • Use third-party authentication providers and services like SAML, OpenID Connect, or Kerberos.
  • Preserve the integrity of your data with message authentication and SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Block unwanted traffic with traffic filter.
  • Secure your settings with the Elasticsearch keystore.

In addition, we also enable encryption at rest (EAR) by default. ec supports EAR for both the data stored in your clusters and the snapshots we take for backup, on all cloud platforms and across all regions.

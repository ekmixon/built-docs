Anomaly Detection with Machine Learningedit

For Free Trial and Platinum subscription deployments, Machine Learning functionality is available throughout Elastic Security. You can view the details of detected anomalies in the Anomalies table shown on the Hosts, Network and associated details pages. You can drag and drop anomaly details to Timeline, such as the Entity itself, or any of the associated Influencers.
